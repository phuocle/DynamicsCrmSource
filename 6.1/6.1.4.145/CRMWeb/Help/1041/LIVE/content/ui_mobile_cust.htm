Customize CRM for phones

  1. Make sure you have the System Administrator security role or equivalent permissions in Microsoft Dynamics CRM.

    Check your security roles

    1. Follow the steps in View your user profile.

    2. Don't have the correct permissions? Contact your system administrator.

  2. Follow the steps for the app you're using.

    If using the CRM web application

    1. On the nav bar, click or tap Microsoft Dynamics CRM > Settings. Then click or tap Customization > Customize the System.

    If using CRM for Outlook

    1. In the Navigation Pane, click or tap Settings > Customization > Customizations. Then click or tap Customize the System.

  3. In the Navigation Pane, click or tap Settings > Customizations, and then click or tap Customize the System.

  4. Under Components, expand Entities.

  5. Click the entity you want to enable in Microsoft Dynamics CRM for phones, and under Outlook & Mobile, select the CRM for phones check box.

    Not all Microsoft Dynamics CRM entities are available in CRM for phones.

    Note

    Consider the screen size and usage goals for your users when you evaluate which entities and attributes you want to make available to mobile devices.

  6. Expand the entity that you want to enable in CRM for phones, and then click Forms.

  7. Double-click or tap the form with a Form Type of Mobile.

  8. Select the attributes you want to be visible on the CRM for phones form from the Available Attributes list, and then click Add.

    Required attributes are automatically listed in the Selected Attributes list; they cannot be removed.

  9. (Optional) Change the order in which the entities are listed by using the Move Up and Move Down buttons.

    Note

    When you define and organize CRM for phones attributes, consider which attributes users will use most frequently and place them at the top.

  10. Click or tap Save and Close.

  11. To publish all entities, on the Actions toolbar, click Publish All Customizations.

    --OR--

    To publish selected entities, under Components, expand Entities. Click or tap the specific entity, and on the Actions toolbar click or tap Publish.

    Caution

    When you publish customizations, remember that there is only one "master" publishing mechanism that always publishes for Microsoft Dynamics CRM and CRM for phones at the same time. Using the Publish All button in CRM for phones publishes all changes to not only CRM for phones entities, but also publishes any pending changes to the same entities in the main environment. The reverse is also true. Publishing in Microsoft Dynamics CRM also publishes entities in CRM for phones.

  12. After you customize CRM for phones, you must provide the following access information to your users:

    1. Access CRM for phones by using a mobile Web browser to navigate to https://<CRM_server>/m where <CRM_server> is the path to your server. Or, http://<organization name>.crm.dynamics.com for Microsoft Dynamics CRM Online.

    2. Sign in using a valid account.
