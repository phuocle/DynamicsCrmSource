Create a quick case record

If a case record for the issue the customer is calling for doesn't already exist, you can create a quick case record right where you are in the process.

  1. In the case record that you're working on, for Find Case, click the lookup icon, and then click +New.

    An inline form opens with limited fields of the case form. This enables you to record some quick details of the case without the need to open another form.

  2. Specify the following details, and click OK:

    • Title

    • Subject. We recommend that you specify a subject for a case. When you are researching for the case, the Find Similar Cases pop-up form lists all the relevant cases that have the same subject as the one selected in this field.

    • Priority

    • Owner

    This case is automatically associated with the customer you've selected in the Find Customer lookup field.
