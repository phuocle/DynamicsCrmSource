Create or edit a rollup query

Rollup queries help you gather data about a given parent goal, including all the child goals under it, in one view.

Can I do this task?

This task requires a manager, vice president, CEO-Business Manager, System Administrator, or System Customizer security role or equivalent permissions. More information about specific permissions and performing this task while offline: Sales Permissions

  1. On the Navigation Pane, click or tap Sales, and then click or tap Rollup Queries.

  2. In the Records group, click or tap New.

    - OR -

    To edit a rollup query, select it and click or tap Edit.

  3. Under Information, click or tap General, and then enter information or observe any noted restrictions or requirements as needed:

    • Name

      Required. Enter between 1 and 50 characters.

    • Entity Type

      Required. This determines the criteria you can use later to build your query.

      Tip

      If you change this selection after you have added query criteria in the Query section, your query criteria will be deleted.

    • Owner

      Required. Click or tap the Lookup button and select the user or team who will own this query.

  4. Under Query, use the controls to add the criteria for your query. The criteria you can add depends on the entity type you have chosen.

  5. To test the query, click or tap View Records to see the results for the query.

  6. Click or tap Save, Save and Close, or, to create a new rollup query, Save and New.

  7. To view the data for a goal, in the Navigation Pane, click or tap Sales, and then click or tap Goals.

  8. Select the goal you want.

  9. In the Actions group, click or tap Recalculate.

    The values of the actuals are recalculated. In the grid, you can see the recalculated values in the Percentage Achieved, Actual, and In-Progress columns. If you open the goal, under Actuals, you can see the Last Rolled Up Date.
