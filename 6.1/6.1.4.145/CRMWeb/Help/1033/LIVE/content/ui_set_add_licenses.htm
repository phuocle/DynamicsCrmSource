Add user licenses to your subscription

If all of the licenses associated with your subscription have been assigned and additional users need access to Microsoft Dynamics CRM Online, you can add licenses to your subscription.

For additional information about managing Microsoft Dynamics CRM Online licenses, see Manage Microsoft Dynamics CRM Online Licenses

The steps for this task differ depending on where you manage your Microsoft Dynamics CRM Online subscription.

For subscriptions managed through Microsoft online services

In this environment, each user license requires a unique user ID.

  1. Make sure that you have the System Administrator or System Customizer security role or equivalent permissions.

    Check your security role

    1. Follow the steps in View your user profile.

    2. Don't have the correct permissions? Contact your system administrator.

  2. Make sure that you have the Global administrator or Billing administrator security role on the Microsoft Online Services.

    More information: Assigning administrator roles

  3. On the Office 365 admin center page, in the header, click or tap Admin > CRM.

  4. On the select a Microsoft Dynamics CRM Online instance page, under Instance Name, click or tap the entry for the Microsoft Dynamics CRM Online instance you want to add licenses to, and then tap or click Launch CRM.

  5. In Microsoft Dynamics CRM Online, in the Navigation Pane, expand System if necessary, and then tap or click Administration > Subscription Management.

  6. On the Office 365 admin center page, in the left pane, verify that licensing is selected, and then, on the Subscriptions tap, under Subscription, click or tap on the entry for the subscription you want to add licenses to.

  7. On the Subscription details page, click or tap Buy now.

  8. On the Customize your order page, in the text box, enter the total number of user licenses that you want to add, and then click or tap check out.

  9. Follow the directions on the screen to complete your purchase.

Changes to your subscription take a few minutes to take effect and will be reflected in your next month's billing, on a prorated basis.

Important

Signing out of the Microsoft online services portal doesn't sign you out of Microsoft Dynamics CRM Online. You must do that separately.

Note

To reduce the number of licenses in your subscription, contact Support. More information: Contact Support for a billing or subscription issue
